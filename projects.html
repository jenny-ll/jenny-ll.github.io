  • "Clearing" Customs: Institutional Quality and the Environmental Bias of Carbon Trade Flows
    (Undergrad thesis)

    Using an OLS model with country fixed effects and interaction terms, I look at how institutional quality impacts the import trade flows of carbon-intensive goods for 40 countries and over 5,000 products from 2002-2010. My analysis involves cross-sectional comparisons between countries and their behaviour, as well as an attempt to identify a causal relationship between IQ and trade flows through time variation.

    Tools Used: Jupyter Notebook, Python (pandas + numpy), Stata
